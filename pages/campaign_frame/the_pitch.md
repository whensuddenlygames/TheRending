>The planet has been sundered into seven celestial bodies known as Reliquaries, held together by the “Heart of Palaghast,” silver trendrils of light snaking out from a white hot sphere of light. Exemplars, living Relics of the dead god Palaghast now rule what's left Beyond the divine firmaments swirls the Abyssal Horde, daemons who seek the final destruction of the world. While minor deities have arisen wielding self-directed divine power, drawing heretical adherents to their side.
