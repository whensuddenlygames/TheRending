*All classes are available, but some have unique within this setting. Provide the following information to your players.*

## Wizards And Sorcerers
After the Rending a firm distinction has formed between arcane (abyssal magic) and [divine magic](distinctions.md#divine-magic-versus-abyssal-magic). Those wizards and sorcerers who refrain from learning the ways of the Demiurge are granted their knowledge through divine revelation through worship of an [Exemplar](distinctions.md#exemplar).
